How does donating With Phil help?

We're moving closer towards a cashless society, and those sleeping rough are finding it more difficult to get enough money to get back on their feet.

As people lucky enough to benifit from a cashless society and not have these worries, we wanted to make sure that we can still offer a helping hand to those in need.

We partner with charities and local authorities to reach those in need, providing them with a bluetooth beacon and setting up their profile.

Supporters with the app are notified when nearby a person in need, and are able to easily view their profile.

After getting to know their backstory, they can then donate cashlessly right over the app and help spread awareness by sharing their support on social media.

Those sleeping rough are then able to select from our list of partnered establishments where they want to redeem their points, letting them purchase food, housing and more.

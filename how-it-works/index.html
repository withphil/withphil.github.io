How does donating With Phil work?

With Phil is the combination of nifty tech, smart partnerships, and the belief that we can end homelessness together.

READ MORE

1. Distribution

Our organisation distributes a secure bluetooth wearable and phone app to people who are sleeping rough on the streets. We verify that they’re actually homeless through partnering charities or local authorities.

2. Discovery

Once you’ve downloaded Phil, you can discover people in-app when you walk past them and want to donate. Learn their name, their back story, and help them feel valued as a human being.

3. Donation

Choose the amount you want to give: enough to buy a meal, a night in a shelter, a bus ticket, or a counselling session. You can be certain that your donation helps them get back on their feet.

4. Discussion

Share your gifting on social media to raise awareness of homelessness, and encourage your friends to start giving With Phil. Be the change together, so we can wipe out homelessness once and for all.

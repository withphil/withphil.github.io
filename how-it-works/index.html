How does donating With Phil help?

We're moving closer towards a cashless society, and those sleeping rough are finding it more difficult to get enough money for their basic needs, let alone enough to get their lives up and running again.

As people lucky enough to benifit from a cashless society, and not have these worries, we wanted to make sure that we can still offer a helping hand to those in need.

With Phil we can all help those sleeping rough.

READ MORE

Phil lets homeless people receive and redeem cashless donations from the public, through an app.

Donors give support after discovering a person's profile and getting to know their back story.

The donations are made in a token, which can be redeemed for basic essentials, like food and shelter.

Phil also shows the person-in-need the nearest location they can redeem their donations.

The people found on Phil are verified as needing support through partnering charities and local authorities.

Using Phil raises awareness of the homeless, as donors are encouraged to share their giving on social media.
